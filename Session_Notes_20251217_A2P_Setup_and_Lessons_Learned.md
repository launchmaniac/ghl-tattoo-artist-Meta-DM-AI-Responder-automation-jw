### 1. Domain Purchase is Mandatory for A2P Funnel Pages
**Problem:** A2P funnel pages wouldn't go live after snapshot push

**Root Cause:** GHL requires domain/subdomain setup for funnel pages

**Discovery from GHL Support:** No free GHL subdomain exists without purchasing root domain

**Solution:**
- Purchase domain via GHL (~$15/year) OR
- Connect client's existing domain (free if they have one)
- Location: Site ‚Üí Client Portal ‚Üí Domains (non-intuitive, hard to find)

**Cost Impact:**
- Domain: $13 actual ($15 customer-facing)
- A2P Application: $25
- **Total Upfront:** $40 per client setup

**For Future Projects:**
- Ask client: Do you have a domain? (saves $15)
- Purchase domain FIRST before building funnel pages
- Include $40 upfront in onboarding pricing

### 2. A2P Sole Proprietor Identity Verification Process
**Updated Understanding:**
Sole proprietor A2P DOES include identity verification (face scan + ID photo), but it's a separate step AFTER form submission.

**Process:**
1. Complete A2P form (requires Privacy Policy + Terms of Service)
2. Submit application
3. GHL provides identity verification link
4. Send link to client
5. Client completes:
   - Photo of government-issued ID
   - Face scan verification
6. Wait for approval (3-7 days)

**Requirements:**
- Privacy Policy page (SMS consent language)
- Terms of Service page
- Domain for funnel pages
- Client cooperation for identity verification

**Templates:**
A2P messaging for sole proprietor AND business documented in:
https://docs.google.com/document/d/1FItAQ6nWEIGGVPukbHmhHxb3sBts6CmSwLe-SJ4EI7c/edit?tab=t.r1j1ijk7obw5
